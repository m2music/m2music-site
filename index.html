What we do

Everything between the booking and the downbeat.

A production's music department runs on details — contracts, scale rates, benefits, doubles, subs, payroll. We've spent over twenty-five years making sure none of them fall through the cracks.

Music Contracting

We hire and book the orchestra — from the first chair to the last sub — drawing on long relationships with New York's finest musicians and a deep bench across the country for touring productions.

Payroll & Employer of Record

As employer of record, we administer musician payroll under AFM collective bargaining agreements — wages, pension, health benefits, and everything in between — accurately and on time, every week.

Coordination & Consulting

From budgeting an orchestra to navigating union agreements, we work alongside producers, general managers, and music departments to keep the music side of a production running smoothly.

About M²

Built by musicians, for musicians.

M² Music was founded in 1999 by working musicians who knew the business from inside the pit. That perspective still shapes how we work: we understand what it means to be on the other side of the contract, because we've been there.

Today we serve as the music department's quiet infrastructure for some of the biggest titles on Broadway and on tour — handling contracting, payroll administration, and coordination under AFM agreements, with the care and accuracy this work deserves.

We're based in New York, and we answer our own phones.
